The spectacled bear is consider the guardian of forest and moor

Spectacled bears are sacred and magical animals for the indigenous groups in South America. They saw these bears as a guiding spirits, older brothers who linked heaven with earth.
Spectacled bears have a very important role. They are plant seed dispersers and acts as pollinators, carrying pollen on its abundant fur which makes it a very important species for the balance of its environment.
Tremarctos Ornatus is his scientific. His height is between 1.5-2m and his weight is between 140-175kg.
Spectacled bears are diurnal animals, and climb trees in search of food. Although they are omnivorous, their diet is mainly plants and fruits.
the burning of forests for livestock and agriculture have brought these bears closer to the communities. These bears are shy and are not a threat to humans. although due to deforestation and the expansion of livestock, there have been cases of attacks on farm animals.
